Mrs. Smith

Ms. Janet Smith is a Greens Farms Academy faculty member for the Upper School Computer Science department and K-12 STEAM program technical assistant. FTC is a family affair for her; She and her husband, Bob Morse, mentor team 4286 and regularly volunteer for FIRST. They have been known to go watch an FRC match just for fun on the rare occasions they have free time. She is also an FTC parent, proudly supporting the NYC FTC program and watching her daughter’s story unfold with team 9681. Ms. Smith has a habit of founding FTC teams wherever she goes including teams 4326, 7890 and 9681 from the Horace Mann School and team 10791 from Schechter Westchester. When she began working at Greens Farms Academy she happily signed on to help the established and talented team 4286.
